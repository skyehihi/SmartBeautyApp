About Us

Welcome to SmartBeauty!

SmartBeauty is web application connecting local hair and nails salons to their clients through online booking system, hair catalog, and client profile management system. We provide services that helps hair & nail salons develop and expand their business, which includes:

  • Online booking appointment
  • Online payment
  • Client profiling system
  • Communication channels via private chat box and public interactions
  • Marketing campaign
  • Operations reports and forecasts
